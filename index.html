AFROBEATOLOGY™

/ˈafrəʊbiːtlədʒi/ 

 Noun

Def. Study of a style of Modern Nigerian pop music incorporating elements of Hilife, Hip-hop, Reggae, Jazz, Juju, R&B, dancehall and more.

REGISTER NOW

AFROBEATOLOGY is a one day  workshop and networking event in Lagos, Nigeria for aspiring and established musicians, entrepreneurs, producers and  afrobeat enthusiasts to share knowledge and inspiration about the genre by industry insiders brought to you by CCbeat7 Productions. 

 

THE WORKSHOP

Spend the day learning about Nigeria's afrobeat soundscape from music production, entertainment business, branding and PR from the best in Nigeria's music industry.

The workshop ends with a networking party where students aka AFROBEATOLOGISTS  get to connect and share their music with industry insiders.

CheekyChizzy

Chisom Afoamakachukwu Ifeachor aka  CheekyChizzyis a Muscian, Afrobeat Producer and Founder of Afrobeatology. He has been in the Nigerian music scene for over four years. He was an affiliate of DB Records, Produckt Republik and 4000 Degrees Entertainment. 

Afrobeatologist

Each workshop we invite a guest "Afrobeatologist" a well known expert in the Nigerian Afrobeat industry to conduct reviews and share knowledge with workshop participants.

Guest Artist

Students will be introduced to a guest Artist who is making waves in the Nigerian music industry. Participants will get to interact with him or her.
